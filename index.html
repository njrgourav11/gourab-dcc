About Us

Who We Are

  • GourabDDC – Gourab De-Addiction Centre – is a professionally managed rehabilitation facility offering holistic recovery programmes under the umbrella of SR Foundation.
  • Located at Mayuri Vihar, 3rd Lane, Sri Ram Nagar, Berhampur, Ganjam, Odisha – easily accessible from across the region.
  • Reach us at 7008879401 via phone or WhatsApp, or email gourabddc@gmail.com
  • Open 9:00 AM – 1:00 PM & 5:00 PM – 8:00 PM every day.
